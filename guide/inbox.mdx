The Inbox is your command center for focused work. It combines a productivity metrics dashboard with an energy-filtered task list, helping you work on what matters most when you have the energy to do it.

## Deep Work Dashboard

When you open the Inbox, you're greeted with a header showing your Work Area for the current day:

```
Work Area
Friday, Mar 04
```

The dashboard includes three productivity metrics cards:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Productivity Velocity" icon="chart-line">
    Weekly task completion chart showing efficiency trends
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily Focus" icon="zap">
    High-focus tasks completed with circular progress ring
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tasks Done" icon="check">
    Last 5 days completion bar chart with daily goal tracking
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Understanding the Metrics

**Productivity Velocity**
- Shows a week-long trend of task completion
- Displays efficiency percentage vs. previous period
- Example: "+12% Efficiency" badge

**Daily Focus**
- Tracks high-energy tasks completed today
- Shows trend compared to yesterday (e.g., "+25% vs yesterday")
- Visual progress ring toward daily focus goal (default: 3 tasks)

**Tasks Done**
- Mini bar chart of last 5 days
- Today's bar highlighted in cyan (`#06b6d4`)
- Shows progress like "4/8" (completed/goal)

## Energy Filter System

The Inbox's most powerful feature is the energy filter, letting you match tasks to your current energy level:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View the filter bar">
    Look for the "Filter by Energy" section with three pills:
    - **Low Energy** (emerald green)
    - **Neutral** (yellow)
    - **High Focus** (purple)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your energy level">
    Click on the pill matching your current state. The task list updates instantly.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Work on filtered tasks">
    When "High Focus" is selected, the section title changes to "Deep Work" with a "HIGH FOCUS" badge.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Clear the filter">
    Click the "Clear" button to see all tasks again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
By default, the Inbox opens with **High Focus** selected, encouraging you to tackle your most demanding work first.
</Note>

## Task List and Interaction

Below the energy filter, you'll see your filtered task list:

- Each task displays with a checkbox, title, and metadata
- Click the checkbox to mark tasks complete
- Tasks have energy indicators (colored dots) showing their energy requirement
- Empty state message: "No tasks match this filter" when no results

### Task Actions

From the Inbox header, you can:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search">
    Use the search bar to filter tasks by keyword across title and notes.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Notifications">
    Click the bell icon to view task reminders and alerts.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="New Task">
    Click "+ New Task" to open the task creation modal.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## When to Use Inbox vs Other Views

Choose the right view for your workflow:

| View | Best For | Use When |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Inbox** | Deep work sessions | You want to focus on high-energy tasks or need productivity metrics |
| **Today** | Daily execution | You want a simple list of today's scheduled tasks |
| **Upcoming** | Future planning | You need to see what's coming in the next days/weeks |
| **Anytime** | Context switching | You're working on specific projects |
